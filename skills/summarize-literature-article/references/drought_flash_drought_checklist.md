# 干旱与骤旱文献专项检查表

Use this reference when the article is about drought, flash drought, agricultural drought, meteorological drought, hydrological drought, ecological drought, vegetation stress, land-atmosphere coupling, or climate extremes.

## 研究问题识别

Check whether the article focuses on:

- Drought monitoring, detection, classification, propagation, attribution, prediction, risk, vulnerability, or impact assessment.
- Flash drought onset, intensification rate, duration, recovery, spatial spread, compound heat-drought events, or early warning.
- Drought effects on soil moisture, evapotranspiration, vegetation productivity, crop yield, ecosystem carbon/water fluxes, streamflow, groundwater, or wildfire risk.

## 定义与指标

Record the exact drought definition and threshold. Common indicators include:

- Meteorological: SPI, SPEI, precipitation percentile, precipitation deficit.
- Agricultural/soil moisture: soil moisture percentile, SMP, SMDI, root-zone soil moisture anomaly.
- Evaporative stress: ESI, EDDI, ET/PET anomaly, vapor pressure deficit.
- Vegetation/ecological: NDVI, EVI, SIF, GPP, LAI, VCI, VHI, NIRv.
- Hydrological: SSI, streamflow percentile, runoff anomaly, groundwater anomaly.
- Flash drought: rapid intensification threshold, pentad/weekly rate, onset duration, percentile drop, combined soil moisture and evaporative demand criteria.

Evaluate whether the chosen index matches the drought type, study scale, ecosystem, and research question.

## 数据重点

For drought studies, capture:

- Meteorological forcing: precipitation, temperature, radiation, wind, humidity, VPD, PET.
- Land surface variables: soil moisture layers, evapotranspiration, runoff, snow, soil temperature.
- Remote sensing: MODIS, Landsat, Sentinel, GRACE/GRACE-FO, SMAP, ESA CCI SM, GLEAM, FLUXCOM, SIF products.
- Reanalysis/model data: ERA5/ERA5-Land, GLDAS, MERRA-2, NLDAS, CMIP, regional climate simulations.
- Ground truth: station observations, flux tower data, stream gauges, crop yield, field measurements.
- Ancillary data: land cover, irrigation, soil texture, elevation, aridity index, population/cropland exposure.

Check temporal resolution carefully. Flash drought studies often require pentad, weekly, or daily data; monthly data may smooth onset and intensification.

## 方法创新判断

Highlight innovation when the paper:

- Proposes or improves a flash drought identification rule.
- Combines soil moisture decline with atmospheric demand or vegetation response.
- Links drought onset to drivers using causal, attribution, machine learning, or process-based methods.
- Resolves scale mismatch between satellite, reanalysis, station, and model outputs.
- Adds independent validation with observations, flux towers, yield, streamflow, or event records.
- Separates drought onset, development, persistence, recovery, and impact stages.
- Builds operational monitoring, forecasting, or warning products.

## 结果解释重点

Look for:

- Spatial hotspots and regional heterogeneity.
- Seasonal differences and phenological constraints.
- Trend, frequency, duration, severity, and intensity changes.
- Driver contributions: precipitation deficit, heatwave, VPD, radiation, soil moisture memory, vegetation feedback, circulation patterns, land use, irrigation.
- Propagation from meteorological to agricultural, ecological, or hydrological drought.
- Compound or cascading impacts.

## 常见不足

Common limitations to check:

- Threshold sensitivity not tested.
- Index cannot capture local drought mechanism.
- Validation is absent or depends only on reanalysis.
- Spatial resolution is too coarse for cropland, basin, or ecosystem heterogeneity.
- Irrigation and land management are ignored.
- Short records cannot separate trend from climate variability.
- Vegetation response is lagged but treated as synchronous.
- Flash drought onset is detected from data too coarse to support rapid intensification claims.
- Uncertainty from PET method, soil moisture depth, cloud contamination, or model bias is not quantified.
